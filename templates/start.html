Sane Grid

The subscription grid for
people who like to stay sane

If you have a lot of subscriptions on YouTube, you know that it's hard to keep your sanity with the current interface, let alone the constant stream of user-hostile changes coming your way.

Now, you could use a browser script to make the horrible "feeds" page a little easier on the eye… and always play catch-up with YouTube, or you could use YT Sanegrid.

Currently updating to the YouTube v3 API. Sorry, but stuff will be broken for a little while.

Here is why it's better than a browser script:

  • It has a minimal, pleasant, full-screen interface
  • It never forgets a video once it's in your subscription inbox
  • You can mark an item as watched or hide it with one click
  • It has advanced features like automatic filters based on keywords
  • It's actively developed and if you have a good idea, I'll put it in
